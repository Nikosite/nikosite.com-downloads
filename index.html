Jesse Jackson Discography [Update log 21-06-26: New album!!!!!]
Jesse Jackson is a revolutionary artist who originates from New York City. Born in 2029, he was growing up adopted in the city's slums, he used to rule a small gang to prey on wall street yuppies.
With so much violence he witnessed, he found himself by making music. His main inspirations were Devon Hendryx and Kanye West. Before his debut album, Jesse Jackson made a song "Bloat" and sold it out through selling Zyme.
He self-released it on Bandcamp for free, then the song was published in Spotify and iTunes. It was a hit for the entire East Coast. After he made millions on this song, he and Kurt Kingston recorded an album together.
Kurt didn't ask for the credit so it's all about Jesse Jackson, which is the point of this album. The self-titled album was self-published in 2052, on Bandcamp (for 33.33$), on Spotify, on iTunes, but not on SoundCloud. Kurt claimed that SoundCloud is "a platform for bums".
Okay but in all seriousness, there are just mixes of my favourite songs, yes even the wind SFX.
